Frequently Asked Questions

Which assets can I use as collateral?

We currently support Ethereum or Polygon NFTs, ERC-20 tokens, game items and in-app credits. New chains are added continuously.

How much can I borrow?

LTV ratios range from 20â€“60 % depending on asset volatility and your on-chain credit score.

How does the insurance work?

An automatic policy protects the lender up to 80 % of the loan amount if collateral value drops or repayment is missed.

What are the fees?

The platform charges 2.5 % of the loan amount plus an insurance premium (~1 %), shared with the lender.

Can I repay early?

Yes. Interest accrues daily, so you pay only for the days you use the loan.

What happens if collateral value crashes?

The smart contract triggers a partial liquidation to keep the lender covered, while insurance tops up any remaining gap.
